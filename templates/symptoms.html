Symptoms

  • In General, Arrhytmia symptoms are

    • Shortness of breath
    • A fluttering in the chest
    • A slow heartbeat (bradycardia)
    • A racing heartbeat (tachycardia)
    • Chest pain

  • May also include

    • Anxiety
    • Fatigue
    • Lightheadedness or dizziness
    • Fainting (syncope) or near fainting
    • Sweating
